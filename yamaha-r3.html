Info

The Yamaha YZF-R3 is a compelling sport motorcycle that effortlessly blends performance and style. Powered by a responsive 321cc parallel-twin engine, the R3 delivers a thrilling riding experience, striking a balance between power and agility. Its lightweight frame and advanced suspension ensure nimble handling, making it an ideal choice for both urban commuting and spirited rides. The R3's sleek design, featuring dual headlights and a sharp fairing, mirrors the aggressive aesthetic of its larger YZF-R siblings. With a focus on precision engineering and modern technology, the Yamaha YZF-R3 stands as a dynamic and visually appealing option for riders seeking an exhilarating two-wheeled adventure.

Racing

The Yamaha R3 dominated the inaugural FIM Supersport 300 World Championship in 2017, securing Rider, Team, and Manufacturer's titles. With Yamaha's support and development, the R3 continued its supremacy in 2018, offering legendary style, advanced technology, and thrilling performance typical of the R-series. As a lightweight A2-category Supersport machine, it provides a perfect platform for new talents, aged 15 and up, to showcase their potential on the international stage. The 300cc inline twin-cylinder engine, combined with a light chassis and performance racing kit, enables young entrants to refine their competitive skills on the path to becoming future WorldSBK champions.
